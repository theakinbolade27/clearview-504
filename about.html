ABOUT CLEARVIEW 💡

clearview was built by a 500L BMT student at FUTA to serve two purposes: helping visually impaired users access information freely, and helping BMT 504 students study and pass their exams.

Every page is designed with accessibility first large text, high contrast, and screen reader support.
